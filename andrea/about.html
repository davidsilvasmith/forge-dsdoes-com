About Andrea

The work has always been about people.

Long before coaching had a name here, Andrea was helping people navigate pressure, planning, and possibility. Her career has always centered on building stronger human foundations.

After graduating from Michigan State University, she built early experience in talent and workforce development, helping people think more clearly about growth, alignment, and next steps. In 2017, she made a deliberate shift into financial services after recognizing a growing gap in trustworthy guidance and practical support.

That move was never just about money. It was about stress, stability, and making sure people left conversations with more confidence than they brought in.

Her lens

Planning is a form of care.

Andrea’s philosophy is simple and powerful: it is easier to change a plan than not have one. That belief shows up in every conversation. You do not need to have it all figured out to begin. You just need a grounded starting point.

She listens first.

Especially for women who are used to being interrupted, dismissed, or coached over, Andrea creates a space that feels collaborative from the first minute.

She thinks structurally.

Her background in economic development and strategic planning means she naturally sees systems, patterns, and leverage points others miss.

She moves toward usefulness.

No empty inspiration. No performative self-help. The goal is always a better relationship with your time, energy, decisions, and direction.

East Lansing roots

Deeply grounded in the Greater Lansing community.

Andrea is an East Lansing resident whose work has reached far beyond individual roles. Through leadership with the Lansing Economic Development Corporation and broader community planning efforts, she has contributed to the thinking behind regional growth, revitalization, and long-term resilience.

That local credibility matters. It signals steadiness, commitment, and an ability to navigate complexity without losing sight of the people inside it.

Serving East Lansing, the Michigan State University community, and professionals across Greater Lansing and Michigan.
Just as she has helped build stronger community infrastructure, Andrea now helps clients build stronger personal infrastructure.

More clarity. Better boundaries. Smarter decisions. Less reactive living.

If you’re ready to stop carrying it alone, this is your place to start.

Coaching with Andrea is practical, warm, and built for real life.
